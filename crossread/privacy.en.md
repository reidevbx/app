# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 1, 2026**

Welcome to CrossRead! We value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
**We do not collect any personal data.**

CrossRead is a utility application designed to help users convert articles into crossword puzzles using AI technology.
- We do not require you to register an account.
- We do not track your personal identity or location.
- The article links you input are used solely for generating game content in real-time; we do not store this content.

## 2. Third-Party Services
To provide AI generation features, we may use third-party AI model services. The content you input may be sent to these services for processing, strictly for the purpose of generating the game.

## 3. Data Security
Since we do not store your personal data, there is no risk of data breach regarding your personal information. All processing is done on your device or via real-time cloud computing and is discarded immediately after processing.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
